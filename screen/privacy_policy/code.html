1. Operational Telemetry Usage

In our "Abyssal Tech" stack, telemetry is more than just data—it is the heartbeat of the cold chain. We utilize IoT sensor data (GPS, Temp, Humidity, Light) exclusively to ensure the safety of transit and to provide real-time updates through your Dashboard. We do not sell this operational data to third-party marketing firms.

2. Seller Verification Protocol

To maintain the "Abyssal Standard," all sellers must undergo a rigorous identity verification process. This includes biometrics (where permitted), facility inspections, and financial auditing. This data is stored in air-gapped encrypted volumes, accessible only during the onboarding and quarterly review periods.

3. GDPR & The Right to be Forgotten

Clients may request the permanent deletion of their account data. While we must retain certain financial records for up to 7 years to comply with international maritime and trade laws, all personal identifiers will be purged from our active logistics and telemetry databases within 30 days of a valid request.
